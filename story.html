Our Story

Intrepid Brass, founded in 2016, started as a brass quintet in North Carolina and has since expanded into a collection of brass professionals who come together for small and large ensemble events across the country. Current quintets reside in Wichita, KS and Charlotte, NC.

Many of our musicians are talented at playing a variety of intruments, however Intrpid Brass band specializes in these five in particular...

  1. Trumpet
  2. French Horn
  3. Tuba
  4. Trombone
  5. Percussion
